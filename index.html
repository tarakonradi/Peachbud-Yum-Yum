Bringing Fido in the Plane

To bring your pet in the cabin may seem daugnting, if not impossible, but it's not so bad!

Follow these steps to ensure a smooth ride:
  1. Make sure the airline allows pets- click here to see a list with prices!
  2. Pick out your flight
  3. Call the airline to ensure there's not more than 2 pets on the flight already (most airlines only allow 2 per flight-check with them to be sure)
  4. Once you have confirmed there's less than two pets, book your flight and then call them back to confirm you are brining your pet in the cabin
  5. If there's more than two you just have to be the first to check in at the airport. You cannot check in prior to the airport because you have a pet. At the airport you will check in and then be re-directed to a different line to pay the pet fee
  6. Make sure you have a good carrier for your pet and be prepared to have your little love bug under the seat during take-off and landing
  7. After checking in and paying the pet fee head to the plane, board, respect others might not like your furry guy, and enjoy the flight
If you're like me and can't afford that pet fee all the time AND happen to be deathly afraid of flying despite the fact your husband is a pilot... you can make your pet an emotional support pet
